# Arabic Summary Rule

After completing any task or substantial unit of work, you must provide a concise summary of what was accomplished in Arabic.

## Instructions

1. **Format**: Provide the summary at the end of your final response (via `notify_user` or final message).
2. **Content**: Briefly explain:
    * What was changed or implemented.
    * Any important design decisions (colors, layout).
    * Verification results (if applicable).
3. **Language**: Modern Standard Arabic (Fusha) or a professional tech-oriented Arabic.

## Example
>
> **ملخص العمل:**
> قمت بإصلاح مشكلة البحث في صفحة الكلمات وتحديث تصميم زر "إضافة".
